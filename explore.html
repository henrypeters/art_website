What Is Art ?

Art is a universal topic. It's a topic that everyone understands. Some might say that it is the expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture, producing works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power.

But it can also be defined as the expression that comes from our inner mind

Why It's Important

It's important because;

  • it helps us feel free, refreshed, and exhilarated
  • it allows us see new perspectives on our world, our emotions and our possible future
  • it guides us on powerful journeys in ways that are often safer than real life, but which can impact our real lives positively nonetheless.

Branches Of Arts

Some branches of art

Art is divided into many branches. They include visual and non-visual Art. Visual art are artwork that we can physically touch and see, examples are fine arts and applied arts. While non-visual arts are actions that we can only feel with our emotions. They include, performing art, liberal art and language art

Professions In Art

Some profesions in art

I'll like to ask a question, what is profession? Well, profession is the work in which one is good at and he/she earns his/her income from it

An artist's main assignment is to create something interesting, unique or functional for use, for sale or exhibition, or just to entertain. There are many types of artists and each type of art has its own job description. The professions in art are; graphic designer, photographer, animator, sculptor, ceramist etc

Colours

What is colour

Colour is seen by artists as a kind of pigments applied to a surface or design to make it more appealing and attractive

Science has shown that colour is present in light.Some remarkable discoveries on colour theory was made by a great scientist called Sir Isaac Newton. Colour is an essential element of art, which adds beauty to the work of art and make them more attractive.There are seven colours in the rainbow. They include; red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet
